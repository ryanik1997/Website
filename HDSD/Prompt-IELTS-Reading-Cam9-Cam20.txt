GROUP TYPES:
  tfng + questions type true-false-not-given (TRUE/FALSE/NOT GIVEN)
  ynng + questions type yes-no-not-given (YES/NO/NOT GIVEN) — views of WRITER
  matching-headings + headings[] + questions per paragraph
  matching-paragraph + paragraphLetters A–G
  matching-features + features[]
  multiple-choice — options A/B/C/D
  gap-fill | sentence-completion | summary-completion (+ wordBank nếu chọn từ)

RULES:
- answer: lowercase (true, not-given, yes, ii, a…)
- explanation: tiếng Việt ngắn
- passage[]: GIỮ label A/B/C khi đề có; không gộp paragraph
- KHÔNG placeholder
- Đủ số câu theo range passage
